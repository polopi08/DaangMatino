Help make our roads safer by reporting potholes, damaged signs, and other road hazards in real-time.

Smart Algorithms Behind the System

Our system uses smart algorithms to improve road maintenance planning and make better use of resources

Knapsack Problem

Used to select a subset of roads that can be repaired within a limited time, based on severity and repair duration

Dijkstra's Algorithm

Identifies optimal alternative routes for commuters when main roads are under maintenance

Quick Sort

Sorts the selected roads by severity and other factors to prioritize the repair order
