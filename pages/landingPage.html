HOW IT WORKS

Knapsack Algorithm

Used to select a subset of roads that can be repaired within a limited time, based on severity and repair duration.

Dijkstra's Algorithm

Identifies optimal alternative routes for commuters when main roads are under maintenance.

Quick Sort

Sorts the selected roads by severity and other factors to prioritize the repair order.

ABOUT US

This website is developed by students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP), under the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program, as part of their final requirement for their Course Design and Analysis of Algorithms. The team is composed of dedicated individuals committed to delivering a functional and purposeful digital solution that addresses the need for an efficient road network maintenance planning system. With their shared aspiration of change and quality output, the group has demonstrated their teamwork, technical expertise, and understanding of real-world applications through this project. With this collective effort, the website DaangMatino was created as the final product.
